Books

This isn't really a project, at least not in the traditional sense that I think of projects. However, I thought someone might find value in this, and that I could use it as a reference for myself. This is a growing document of books I've read and that I feel you should, or shouldn't read. Enjoy! If you'd like to discuss any of them, I'd love to hear from you.

  • Blood Meridian - Cormac McCarthy

  • Stuff Matters - Mark Miodownik
  • The Headspace Guide to Meditation - Andy Puddicombe
  • Educated: A Memoir - Tara Westover
  • 21 Lessons for the 21st Century - Yuval Noah Harari
  • Good To Great - Jim Collins
  • Animal Farm - George Orwell
  • Lord of the Flies - William Golding
  • The Snowball Warren Buffett and the Business of Life - Alice Schroeder
  • A Concise Guide to Macro Economics - David A. Moss
  • Cat's Cradle - Kurt Vonnegut
  • Player Piano - Kurt Vonnegut
  • Sirens of Titan - Kurt Vonnegut
  • Galpagos - Kurt Vonnegut
  • Slaughterhouse Five - Kurt Vonnegut
  • Cloud Atlas - David Mitchell
