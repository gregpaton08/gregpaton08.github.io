Problem

Ever wonder what the temperature was inside your house versus outside your house? I wondered all the time. Should I have my window fan on? Which way should it blow, in or out? So, I created a simple app to feed me that info.

Solution

Weather Station gives me the simple data that I need with no distractions. Plus, I can access it from anywhere and see what the temperature inside my house is currently sitting at.
